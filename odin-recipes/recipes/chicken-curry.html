Chicken Curry

Description

A fragrant and mildly spiced chicken curry with onions, tomatoes, and warm spices. Adjust heat to taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken thighs, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped (or 1 cup canned)
  • 2 tsp curry powder (or garam masala)
  • 1 cup coconut milk (optional)
  • Salt and oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil and saut√© onions until translucent.
  2. Add garlic and ginger; cook 1 minute.
  3. Add spices and toast briefly to release aroma.
  4. Add chicken pieces; brown on all sides.
  5. Stir in tomatoes and simmer until chicken is cooked through.
  6. Add coconut milk if using and simmer 5 minutes. Adjust seasoning and serve with rice.
