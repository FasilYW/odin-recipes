Fluffy Pancakes

Description

Light and fluffy pancakes perfect for a weekend breakfast. Serve with syrup, fresh fruit, or your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Whisk milk, egg, and melted butter in another bowl.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixedâ€”small lumps are okay.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake; cook until bubbles form, flip and cook until golden.
  6. Serve warm with toppings.
