Lasagna

Description

A classic layered Italian dish with rich meat (or vegetable) sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta, and melted cheese. This lasagna is comforting, ideal for family dinners and can be prepared ahead.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles (or use no-boil)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1 1/2 cups ricotta or béchamel
  • 1 lb ground beef (or mixed vegetables)
  • 24 oz tomato pasta sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook noodles according to package (if using regular noodles).
  3. Brown the ground beef with a little olive oil; drain any excess fat.
  4. Stir in the tomato sauce and simmer 10 minutes; season to taste.
  5. Assemble: a thin layer of sauce, noodles, ricotta, meat sauce, mozzarella; repeat for 3 layers.
  6. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
  7. Bake 25–30 minutes until bubbly and golden. Let rest 10 minutes before serving.
